Block Breaker

Destroy every block to win! Lives: 3

Controls: Left / Right arrows to move. Space to launch. R to restart.

Instructions / Description

Move the platform left and right with your arrow keys. Press Space to launch the ball. Destroy every block to advance to the next level. Each level changes the background colour and slightly increases ball speed. Beat level 3 to win the game!
